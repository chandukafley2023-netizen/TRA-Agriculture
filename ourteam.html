Meet the Innovators

We are a dedicated group of students at TRA combining computer science, botany, and engineering to build sustainable agricultural systems for our school campus.

The Core Team

⚙️

Automation Team

Code & Sensors

Handles the real-time dashboard, Firebase database integration, and the automated hardware systems that monitor our crops.

🧪

Nutrition Team

Plant Health

Manages daily nutrient dosing, monitors pH and EC levels, and ensures our plants receive the exact chemical balance they need to thrive.

🏗️

Structure Team

Hardware & Build

Designs and maintains the physical hydroponic systems, deep water culture beds, PVC piping, and the physical greenhouse frameworks.

📝

Documentation Team

Media & Logs

Records crop yields, updates the website, takes progress photos, and writes the detailed reports on our agricultural experiments.
