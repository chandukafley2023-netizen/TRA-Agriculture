Growing the Future, Today.

Welcome to our school's agricultural initiative! We are blending technology, biology, and sustainability to grow fresh produce right here on campus. Explore our different growing environments and see what we're cultivating this season.

Our Growing Facilities

🥬 Twin Greenhouses

We operate two state-of-the-art greenhouses dedicated to our hydroponic systems. By growing without soil, we save water and maximize space. These climate-controlled environments allow us to grow fresh leafy greens, tomatoes, and herbs year-round, regardless of the weather outside.

🍄 The Mushroom Hut

Step into the dark and humid environment of our dedicated Mushroom Hut! Here, we cultivate various strains of edible fungi. This facility teaches us about decomposition, humidity control, and alternative, highly sustainable food sources.

🌻 Outdoor Garden

While we love high-tech farming, we haven't forgotten our roots. Our traditional outdoor soil garden focuses on seasonal crops, composting, and supporting local pollinators. It's the perfect place to get our hands dirty and learn about soil ecology.
