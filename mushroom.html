Hut Technology & Sensors

🌫️

Ultrasonic Foggers

Mushrooms don't have skin to trap moisture, so they can dry out easily. We use industrial ultrasonic foggers tied directly to our live dashboard's humidity sensors to keep the air at a constant 85-95% relative humidity.

💨

CO2 Exhaust System

Unlike plants, fungi inhale oxygen and exhale carbon dioxide (CO2). If CO2 levels get too high, the mushrooms grow long, stringy stems. Our automated exhaust fans trigger whenever CO2 surpasses 1000 ppm.
